About me...

I'm a full stack ninja with  degree in chemical sciences. I took up web development as a part time hobby and it stuck so well I decided to make a big career change: from research in chemistry to creating responsive easy-to-use web applications and pages.

I have experience in HTML, CSS and JavaScript by working on the projects using the different available frameworks, you can find an example of those in my portfolio. My goal is to develop responsive, well designed and user friendly programmes - which you can see my scrolling through this page as well as seeing some of my other work!

  1. Responsive Design - At the core of all of my work is one simple idea - responsive design. I don't believe there is a point in building applications unless you are willing to dedicate your time and energy in ensuring that it is compatible with all devices and that it is fully responsive.
  2. Ease and Simplicity - My second priority in designing web pages and applications is to ensure that they are easy to use and that they are simple. This means that I will spend time making sure that my code is concise and to the point and that every single line of code matters. This means that the end result will render quickly and in turn will provide the user with a pleasant and easy experience. 
  3. Design - After ensuring that my work is responsive and to the point, I focus on the deisgn of the page. UI is one of the most important aspect of web development and I like to focus extra time to ensure that the design is sharp and easy on the eye.
